Called Jodi, their real names are Dirk Paesmans and Joan Heemskerk. Jodi was created in 1994. As 2 internet artists working together in the Netherlands, their most well-known work is wwwwwwwww.jodi.org. Becoming some of the first artists to make web art, they became pretty well known. They have many other websites besides their most famous one.
